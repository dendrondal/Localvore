Description

An easy noodle bowl filled with roasted broccoli and an easy peanut sauce. Perfect for a quick lunch or light dinner.

  • 3 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon fresh minced ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons honey (or favorite vegan sweetener)
  • Sriracha, as desired for heat

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425Ëš. Chop broccoli florets into bite-sized pieces and toss with onion and olive oil. Roast for 20 to 25 minutes until broccoli is tender and starting to brown (see note).
  2. While broccoli is roasting, cook noodles according to package. To make the sauce, combine the ingredients in a bowl and stir until smooth.
  3. Once broccoli is done roasting, combine with noodles and sauce. Toss until well combined and serve with a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
