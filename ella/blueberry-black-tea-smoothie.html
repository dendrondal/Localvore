Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed and cooled English breakfast tea
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup peeled, cooked, and chopped red beets (see note)
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons maple syrup
  • 1/4 cup cranberry juice
  • 4 ice cubes
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Combine ingredients in a blender in the order listed. Blend until smooth and add more ice if desired. Taste and adjust sweetness by adding more maple syrup.
