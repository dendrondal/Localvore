Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons sesame seeds
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • ¬º teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons minced cilantro

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Àö. Cut turnips into ¬º‚Äù cubes and place in a bowl. Add minced garlic, olive oil, sesame seeds, honey, and sea salt: toss until well combined. Spread turnips out into a single layer in a roasting pan and bake for 25-30 minutes until caramelizing and turnips are tender.

  3. Serve turnips over cooked barley.
