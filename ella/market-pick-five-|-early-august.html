Market Pick Five | Early August

As promised, another new series I’m starting on the blog! I’m really excited about this one because it harkens back to one of the main reasons I stuck with Naturally Ella about four years ago. I wasn’t really sure what this space was going to be and then I joined an amazing CSA. Each week I’d photograph and share what was growing. It was, by far, one of the highlights in my early blogging ‘career’. That part of the blog slowly faded out but I wanted to bring it back with a slightly different spin involving the farmers’ market. Every other week, I’ll be sharing five market picks for your weekend along with a few recipes to make with each.

I’d love to hear what your excited about this week and any recipes/ideas you might be making!

Right now this week is all about hanging on to summer for just a bit longer, so my picks are:
